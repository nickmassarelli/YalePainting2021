Chibụike Ụzọma
Chibụike Ụzọma (b.1992 Port Harcourt, Nigeria) is an artist whose multidisciplinary practice treats art as a subject-object in and of itself by bartering narrative and interpretation with process and subjective experience. Through material and sensory signals, he explores possibilities in imagery­—making subject matter a pretext for activity, and context a fluid ground.
