“I asked myself

What, Sappho, can
you give one who
has everything,
like Aphrodite?”

–Sappho
Jonathan Rajewski
(b. 1986, Bismarck, ND) is an artist and writer living between Detroit, Michigan and New Haven, Connecticut. He received a BA in Philosophy from Michigan State University in 2009 and is a 2021 MFA Candidate in Painting at the Yale School of Art. He has exhibited at Nina Johnson Gallery, Miami, FL; Marlborough Gallery, New York, NY; Jack Hanley Gallery, New York, NY; Reyes Finn Gallery, Detroit, MI; and the Museum of Contemporary Art Detroit. His writing has appeared in Mousse Magazine, The Exhibitionist (MIT Press), Essay’d (Wayne State Press), and Three Fold.
