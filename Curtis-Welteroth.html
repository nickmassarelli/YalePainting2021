Exhibition Artists
Curtis Welteroth
Through a range of media and styles, Curtis Welteroth deconstructs the histories and contemporaneity of the horror genre to reveal the machinations of queer cultural transgression and marginalization. Their work implicates viewers in moments of both metaphorical and literal investigation through an embrace of the paranormal, the intimate, the architectural, and the linguistic to interrogate the boundaries between self and space.
