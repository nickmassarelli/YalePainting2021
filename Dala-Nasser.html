Dala Nasser
I abstained from showing work in this years thesis show in mourning of the assassination of Lokman Slim.

As a friend of lokmans, and a Lebanese citizen, I find it paramount to exercise my right as an artist with a voice to choose silence in moments where words fail, where solidarity is needed, and abstain from exhibiting in mourning of this truly horrendous crime. I hope this gesture carries some of the weight that this incredibly courageous individual lived by.
