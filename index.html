The Yale School of Art is pleased to announce the Painting and Printmaking Graduate Thesis show:

IN PRAISE OF SHADOWS

Due to Covid-19 precautions the physical exhibition in Green Hall Gallery is viewable only to members of the Yale community, who are invited to make appointments here.

The general public is invited to view a comprehensive online exhibition launching March 1st, on this website.
